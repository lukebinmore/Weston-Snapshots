Weston Snapshots!

Weston-super-Mare's locally led photography club!

Who Are We?

We here at Weston Snapshots are a rag-tag group of individuals with a passion for photography. With members ranging from 18 year old new starters to vets with 50 years of experience. We all love photography and vidiopgrahy and have built a community around that passion!

What Do We Do?

Members of Weston Snapshots meet up regularly, with daily event's planned that a free for everyone to join in with if they would like to. We take walks out to different spots around the Weston-super-Mare area and take photos and videos of things that catch our eye.

How Do I Join?

Head over to the Join Us! page and fill in the simple form to get started. After you have filled in the form, one of our event organisers will get you introduced to the rest of the club, and let you know where we meet!
